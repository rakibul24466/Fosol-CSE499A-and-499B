About Us

  • We are a blockchain-based farming platform that provides a transparent supply chain, decentralized marketplace, weather forecasting, market intelligence, bangla voice command, and training and resources for farmers.

  • Our goal is to help farmers improve the efficiency, transparency, and traceability of their businesses. We do this by providing them with a secure and tamper-proof digital ledger of transactions, as well as access to valuable data and insights.

  • Our platform is designed to be easy to use and affordable, so that even small farmers can benefit from its capabilities. We are committed to helping farmers build a more sustainable and profitable future for themselves and their communities.

  • Here are some of the specific services we offer:
  • Transparent supply chain: We track the movement of food products from the farm to the table, ensuring that consumers know exactly where their food came from and how it was produced.

    • Decentralized marketplace: Farmers can sell their products directly to consumers, without the need for middlemen. This helps them get a better price for their products and reduce costs.
  • Weather forecasting: We collect and share weather data, which helps farmers make better decisions about planting, irrigation, and harvesting.

  • Market intelligence: We collect and analyze market data, such as prices, demand, and supply. This information helps farmers make better decisions about their crops and marketing strategies.

  • Bangla voice command: We provide voice-activated systems that allow farmers to control their farms using their voices. This frees up farmers' time and allows them to focus on other tasks.

  • Training and resources: We provide training and resources on how to use blockchain technology in farming. This helps farmers adopt blockchain technology and reap the benefits it offers.

    • We are committed to providing farmers with the tools and resources they need to succeed. We believe that blockchain technology has the potential to revolutionize the agricultural industry, and we are excited to be a part of that revolution.

  • If you are a farmer or a stakeholder in the agricultural industry, we encourage you to learn more about our platform and how we can help you. We are always looking for ways to improve our services, and we welcome your feedback.
