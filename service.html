Services

  • Transparent supply chain: Blockchain can be used to track the movement of food products from the farm to the table, ensuring that consumers know exactly where their food came from and how it was produced. This can help to improve food safety and reduce fraud.

  • Decentralized marketplace: Blockchain can be used to create a decentralized marketplace where farmers can sell their products directly to consumers, without the need for middlemen. This can help farmers to get a better price for their products and reduce costs.

  • Weather forecasting: Blockchain can be used to collect and share weather data, which can help farmers to make better decisions about planting, irrigation, and harvesting.

  • Market intelligence: Blockchain can be used to collect and analyze market data, such as prices, demand, and supply. This information can help farmers to make better decisions about their crops and marketing strategies.

  • Bangla voice command: Blockchain can be used to create voice-activated systems that allow farmers to control their farms using their voices. This can free up farmers' time and allow them to focus on other tasks.

  • Training and resources: Blockchain can be used to provide farmers with training and resources on how to use blockchain technology in their businesses. This can help farmers to adopt blockchain technology and reap the benefits it offers.
